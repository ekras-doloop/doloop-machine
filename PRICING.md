# Pricing

doloop's metering is being finalized. The principles that hold:

- **No per-seat pricing.** You pay for what doloop does, not how many people use it.
- **Calibration is free.** Reading your codebase to learn its conventions costs nothing.
- **Free = the world · paid = your code · sealed = defensive.** Free checks against
  public-codebase patterns (pooled — you contribute the abstracted lesson back); paid holds
  your code to *its own* conventions; the sealed tier runs hermetic, nothing leaves.
- **Plans:** Free ($0) · Pro ($20/mo, auto-consistency against your own code) ·
  Max ($200/mo, hermetic) · Enterprise (contact us, on-prem + dedicated ratchet).
- **Deterministic & reproducible.** Whatever the meter, it is a number you can reproduce,
  never a black box.
- **BYOL — your model stays yours; in local mode nothing leaves your machine.**
